Our Mission

Habitat for Humanity believes that every person should have a decent, safe, and affordable place to live. We build and repair houses worldwide using volunteer labor and donations. Partner families purchase houses through no-profit, no-interest loans or innovative financing.

Why We Build

There are nearly 2 billion people in slum housing and over 100 million homeless worldwide. We provide shelter and housing assistance to help families recover from natural disasters, war, and civil unrest.
